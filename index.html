Privacy guaranteed

Zero-knowledge architecture

Rclona operates strictly as a local GUI. We have no access to your data. All transfers are executed by the trusted open-source rclone binary on your machine or server.

  • Your files never pass through Rclona’s servers — there are no Rclona cloud servers for your data.
  • Cloud credentials live in your rclone.conf on your host, under your control.
  • The web UI talks only to the local Rclona backend on the same host (or your LAN).
  • Self-hosted Docker means you own the container, volumes, and network boundary.
